12/20/2019

Peg fired the big kiln to Cone 5 with a five minute hold. It took 8:27 minutes to complete. We let the vent run until it should have automatically turned off when the kiln reached 150 degrees.

After letting the kiln cool all night long; from approximately 2:30 in the afternoon until about 7:15 the next morning, it had only cooled to 144 degrees and the items were still hot to the touch.
